DIVERSITY

Sindhura

The rose-red Gulab Khas or Sindhura is unique mango varieties reign the Indian markets.

Baginapalli

This mango variety is produced in Banaganapalle of Kurnool district, Andhra Pradesh. With its pleasant aroma, they are oval in shape, with smooth skin and is around 14 cm in length.

Chausa

It is popular in popular in North India and Bihar. This variety is characterised by incredibly sweet pulp and bright yellow skin.

Malda

Malda, also known as 'King of Mangoes' in Bihar. It is sweet-sour in flavour and is pulpy and delicious.

Kesar

Being among the most expensive varieties, the color of the pulp resembles saffron. It is mostly grown in and around Ahmedabad and Gujarat.

Alphonso

Alphonso is also one of the most expensive varieties of mango, and is grown mainly in Western India and South Gujarat. The Alphonso mango is a seasonal fruit harvested from mid-April through the end of June.
